How ModelChat Works

Welcome to ModelChat! Here's a brief overview of how our platform operates:

For Models
Step 1: Sign Up

Register an account with us and choose the "Model" option. Set up your profile to showcase yourself to fans.

Step 2: Engage in Conversations

Start real-time AI-driven conversations with fans. Interact with multiple fans simultaneously.

Step 3: Customize Your Experience

Fine-tune your digital persona to set a specific style or tone for your AI-driven interactions.

For Fans
Step 1: Sign Up

Register an account with us and choose the "Fan" option. Access the platform and start chatting with models.

Step 2: Engage in Conversations

Engage in real-time conversations with your favorite models, powered by AI.

Step 3: Customize Your Experience

Provide feedback on your chat experiences and customize chat settings to your preference.
